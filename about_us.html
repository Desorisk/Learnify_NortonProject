Our story

Started at a kitchen table, still runs like one

Leanify started in 2026 when three public-school teachers began recording weekend lessons for students who couldn't keep up in crowded classrooms. What began as a shared folder of video lessons grew into a full learning platform, but the intent never changed.

We still build every course around one question: would a real teacher explain it this way? That's why lessons are short, mentors respond within a day, and every learner has someone checking in on their progress.

Today, thousands of learners study with us, guided by mentors who've spent years in real classrooms before joining Learnify.
